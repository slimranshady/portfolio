EB GAMES AD CAMPAIGN

This campaign design was created to promote the new trade deal that EB Games has. Gamers can now trade in their old games in exchange for something new. This project involved making web banners, news paper ads, and bus ads. The challenge of this project was to create a consistent ad that flowed both horizontally and vertically, in multiple locations and sizes.
