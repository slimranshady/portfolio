CHEO ANNUAL REPORT

The main goal of this annual report design was to communicate the information provided to the target audience in a simple, straight forward, and creative manner.

This was achieved with the use of repetitive elementary colour patterns that shift for every section, strong iconography, neatly set typography, and the strategic use of white space.

Sketches

The main theme that carries out throughout this design is a geometric, triangle pattern. The iconography and graphs follow the same theme, which keeps the design consistent and harmonious. The icons are all consistent to one another, yet they each have a unique aspect. This makes the reader curious for what the next icon is going to be. The triangles at the bottom mold a shape onto the white space, which creates good visual flow.

English Half

The document is plastic/comb bound. This allows the reader to stay on a desired spread without constantly having to hold the report down so it stays open. This can be awkward during a meeting. The English version is on one side, and the French on the other, which makes it easy for both parties to view their report.
